Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

ClipboardTyper is designed to work locally on your Mac.

Data We Access

  • Clipboard text only when you trigger typing.
  • Accessibility/event-posting permission to send key events to the active app.

How Data Is Used

  • Clipboard content is used only to simulate typing in the currently focused application.
  • Settings such as shortcut and delay values are saved locally on your device.

Data Sharing and Collection

  • No analytics, telemetry, or advertising SDKs are included by default.
  • No clipboard content is sent to external servers by default.

Retention

  • Clipboard text is read at trigger time and not persisted by default.
  • User preferences are stored locally until removed by uninstall/reset.

Your Choices

  • You can revoke accessibility/event-posting permissions in macOS System Settings at any time.
  • You can stop using the app at any time by quitting or uninstalling it.
