ClipboardTyper

Type clipboard content into the active app after a shortcut and delay. Designed for environments where paste is blocked.

Source (Private) Website Repo

Features

  • Global shortcut trigger
  • Configurable delay before typing
  • Optional delay between keystrokes
  • Menu bar operation with accessibility permission guidance

Privacy

ClipboardTyper runs locally on your Mac. It reads clipboard text only when you trigger typing. No analytics or tracking are included by default.
